Sir Terry's Life in Short

  • 1948 - Born Terence David John Pratchett on 28 April in Beaconsfield, Buckinghamshire, England.
  • 1971 - His first novel, The Carpet People, is published.
  • 1983 - The first Discworld novel, The Colour of Magic, is published.
  • 1998 - Appointed Officer of the Order of the British Empire (OBE).
  • 2007 - Diagnosed with early-onset Alzheimer's disease.
  • 2009 - Knighted for services to literature in the New Year Honours.
  • 2010 - Receives the World Fantasy Award for Life Achievement.
  • 2015 - Dies at the age of 66.
  • 2017 - As per his request, the harddrive containing his unfinished work was destroyed by a steamroller.
